Twilight Fair

4-7pm, 7 November at CBHS

When and Where?

4-7pm, Saturday 7 November 2015

Christchurch Boys' High School

About

The Twilight Fair is a parent initiated fund-raiser for the Japanese Culture and Language trip that is running next year. This trip gives boys from the Year 12 and Year 13 Japanese Language class the opportunity to spend 3 weeks immersed in the culture and language of Japan.

The Twilight Fair will have lots of stalls and activities, and will be a great opportunity to meet others from the community, and to talk with the boys who will be travelling to Japan in April 2016.

Stalls and Activities

  • Main Stalls
    • Trash and Treasure - the garage sale
    • Clothing Stall
    • Book Stall
    • DVDs & CDs, Games (Playstation, Xbox, PC, etc)
    • Car boot and invited stall holders
  • Food Stalls

    • Bacon Butties and Sausage Sizzle
    • Aroha Drinks

  • Activity Stalls
